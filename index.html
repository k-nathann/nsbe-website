Welcome to Our NSBE Chapter

Posted on September 15, 2023 by Chapter President

We are a dedicated chapter of the National Society of Black Engineers committed to increasing the number of culturally responsible Black engineers who excel academically, succeed professionally, and positively impact the community.

Our chapter provides academic excellence programs, professional development, and community outreach to support our members throughout their educational and professional journeys.

We host weekly study sessions, professional development workshops, and community outreach programs throughout the academic year. All students are welcome to participate in our events and activities.

About NSBE

The National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE) is one of the largest student-governed organizations based in the United States. NSBE has more than 600 chapters and nearly 24,000 active members in the U.S. and abroad.
